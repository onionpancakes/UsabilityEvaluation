A Critique of University of Leicester's Website

Purpose: As students of a web design class, our main goal was to learn about what makes a website a good or bad user experience. In the process we hoped to learn about a consistent method of evaluating future websites including our own.

Evaluation Criteria: Our method in completing this project was to evaluate the website from ten different perspectives. We each evaluated the website ten times adhering to Jakob Nielsenâ€™s 10 heuristics, then met up and compared the website ten more times together. This process gave us a very deep and multifaceted view of every aspect of the website.

Results: Overall, exploring the website was a lot of fun and a definite success. The first hand experience of analyzing such a large website made us think critically and expanded our web design knowledge. Compiled on this website are the fruits of our labor, each page represents one of the ten heuristics and goes into detail about what we found. Additionally there are images which show the parts of the website we are talking about, clicking a picture expands it for viewing.
