Visibility of System Status

"The system should always keep users informed about what is going on, through appropriate feedback within reasonable time."

On most of the pages, it shows a breadcrumb near the top left to show users where they are currently, which is useful. However, we also found that certain links will bring you through multiple levels, which maybe be confusing. Additionally, we found that occassionally, some links in the breadcrumb hierarchy lead to invalid, non-existant, or irrelevant pages. Even though the breadcrumb is a good idea and method to show the system status, it should be without bugs to function efficiently.
